Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Who is requesting Google user data?

User data is requested by the Extension.

What data is requesting?

  • YouTube playlists and channels.
  • Files and folders on Google Drive.
  • The user's profile and email.

Why is Google user data requested?

The Extension needs playlists and channels to generate a history of playlist changes. The folders are needed to place files inside. The files are needed to show them to the user in the app as images and text data. The user's profile and email are used by the app to display them to the user on the settings page.

What will it do with user data?

It can:

  • Create, read, update and delete files and folders on Google Drive.
  • Read your and other people's playlists and video info available by your YouTube account.
  • Read your channel.
  • Read your email and profile.
